LooVé

Size : 1410×1200×1000 mm.

Our project, Loo+Love, reimagines the future bathroom as a shared, pet-friendly environment. This concept transforms the restroom into a comfortable and intuitive space for dogs by integrating an organic, grass-lined pad that mimics natural outdoor terrain. The use of natural materials and soft, flowing forms enhances comfort while promoting a sense of connection between pets and owners. The system can also be adapted into a portable version for outdoor events, providing a hygienic and convenient toilet solution for pet owners on the go. This project explores how thoughtful design can bring wellbeing, inclusivity, and practicality into future restroom experiences.
